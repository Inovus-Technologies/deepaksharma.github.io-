Professional Summary

I am a self-driven developer focused on computer science fundamentals and system-level design. My work emphasizes logic-first solutions, explainability, and scalable thinking over quick fixes.

Fueled by Curiosity, Forged by Grit.

My journey into computer science didn't start in a university lecture hall; it started with a detour. Born in Borana and raised in Telangana, my formal education abruptly ended at age 11 due to a single missing signature on a transfer document. The school system closed its doors on me, but my curiosity forced a window open.

I refused to let circumstances define my capability. Without a computer, I spent three years teaching myself programming on a 6-inch Android screen. I didn't just write code; I learned patience, optimization, and how to do more with less.

When I finally acquired a modest, second-hand Lenovo laptop, I didn't just use it—I rebuilt it. I tore it apart, upgraded the hardware myself, and engineered a machine capable of keeping up with my ambition. This hands-on journey taught me that a true engineer doesn't wait for the perfect resources; they build them.

Today, I bring that same relentless problem-solving mindset to every project. I don't just write syntax; I deconstruct problems to their core and build resilient, logic-driven systems.
